Laboratory Safety Cautions

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

  • Always wear safety goggles to protect eyes from chemical splashes and debris
  • Use lab coats to protect skin and clothing from chemical spills
  • Wear closed-toe shoes and avoid loose clothing
  • Use gloves when handling chemicals or biological materials

Chemical Safety

  • Read all chemical labels and safety data sheets carefully before use
  • Never taste, touch, or smell chemicals directly
  • Work in a well-ventilated area, especially with volatile substances
  • Store chemicals properly in designated areas and segregate incompatible substances

Equipment and Workspace Safety

  • Keep workspaces clean and organized
  • Know the location of safety equipment like fire extinguishers, eye wash stations, and emergency showers
  • Handle glassware carefully to prevent cuts
  • Report any damaged equipment immediately
  • Never work alone in the laboratory
  • No eating, drinking, or applying cosmetics in the lab
  • Wash hands thoroughly before leaving the laboratory
