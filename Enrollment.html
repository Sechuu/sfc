Enrollment Procedures

Incoming Freshmen Enrollment Procedure

Complete the online application form
Submit required documents (high school diploma, birth certificate)
Take and pass the entrance examination
Attend the admission interview
Receive admission notification
Pay initial enrollment fees
Attend student orientation program
Register for first-semester classes

Current Freshmen Students Enrollment Procedure

Check academic standing and clearance
Complete online pre-enrollment assessment
Consult with academic advisor
Select courses for upcoming semester
Clear any outstanding balances
Pay tuition and miscellaneous fees
Confirm class schedule online
Obtain student registration form

Old Students Enrollment Procedure

Review academic performance from previous semester
Check enrollment eligibility
Complete online pre-enrollment form
Validate student information
Secure academic clearance
Pay previous semester outstanding balances
Register for upcoming semester courses
Confirm final class schedule

Transferees Enrollment Procedure

Submit transfer application
Provide official transcript of records
Undergo credential evaluation
Take placement examinations if required
Attend transfer student orientation
Consult with academic department
Complete credit transfer assessment
Pay transfer and enrollment fees
Register for appropriate courses
Important: Enrollment deadlines and specific procedures may vary. Always confirm current requirements with the Registrar's Office.
