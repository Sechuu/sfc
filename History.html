History of Saint Ferdinand College-Cabagan


Saint Ferdinand College-Cabagan was founded in 1985 with the goal of providing quality education to the local community. The college started with a small campus and a dedicated team of educators who were passionate about shaping the minds of the next generation.

Over the years, the college has grown significantly, expanding its academic programs and facilities to meet the evolving needs of its students. Today, Saint Ferdinand College-Cabagan is known for its excellence in education and its commitment to nurturing the personal and intellectual growth of its students.

The college takes great pride in its rich history and the countless alumni who have gone on to make their mark in various fields, from business and technology to the arts and sciences. Saint Ferdinand College-Cabagan continues to be a beacon of educational excellence in the region, inspiring students to reach their full potential.
