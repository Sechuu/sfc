About ABM Department

Our ABM program teaches students important business skills, financial knowledge, and how to think like an entrepreneur. The program covers subjects like accounting, management, and marketing to give students a solid understanding of how businesses work.

The program focuses on developing leadership skills and real-world learning, preparing students for careers in the corporate world or for starting their own businesses. We aim to help students become confident decision-makers and problem solvers in the business field.
