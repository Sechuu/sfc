Understanding Human Experience and Social Dynamics

About HUMSS Department

Our HUMSS program helps students explore human behavior, cultural diversity, and social structures. It focuses on understanding how people think, interact, and shape the world around them.

We encourage critical thinking and provide opportunities for students to engage with important social issues. The program empowers students to develop the skills needed to make a positive impact in society, whether in education, social work, or other related fields.
