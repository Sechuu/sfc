Vision

SFC is a dynamic Catholic Educational Institution that develops individuals of competence and character through holistic education and dedicated services to a just and humane society

Mission

Ferdinand College as an evangelizing arm of the church that provides relevant knowledge, enhances practical skills and inculcates Christian values that promotes personal development and social responsibility among people in school and community.

Core Values

  • SPIRITUALITY - Faith in God, Truth, Concern for Others, Care for God's Creation, Social responsibility, Love of Country.
  • TEAMWORK - Unity, Loyalty, Respect for Others, Cooperation, Sharing/Caring.
  • EXCELLENCE - Productivity, Efficiency & Effectiveness, Relevance & Responsiveness, Excellence Delivery of Services, Positive Academic Millieu.
  • PROFESSIONALISM - Accountability, Commitment and Dedication, Competence, Integrity, 5's, Transparency.
  • SERVICE - Efficient and Prompt Delivery of Services, Friendly Atmosphere, Safe and Clean Environment.
