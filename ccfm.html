Inspiring Faith, Community, and Spiritual Growth

About the CCFM

The Campus Christian Formation Ministry (CCFM) is a vibrant community dedicated to fostering the spiritual growth and faith development of students, faculty, and staff at Saint Ferdinand College-Cabagan. Through various programs and initiatives, we aim to create a nurturing environment where individuals can explore their beliefs, deepen their relationship with God, and build meaningful connections with one another.

CCFM designs, coordiantes and implements Christian formation programs and activities ("theory-praxis"), faith in praxis in liturgy/worshipevangilization - education/catechetical, social apostolate that are comprehensive, wholistic/holistic, and inclusive; revelant and contextualized. Concretely, are the following areas and concerns:

1. Faith-life formation, deepening and contextualization (integral faith formation, "faith in context") i.e. liturgical and para-liturgical celebrations, sacraments, recollections, retreats, BEC's, Vocation Mission promotion,etc.

2.Social orientation/Apostle (love of preference for the poor) i.e. outreach programs, exposure-immersion programs, JPIC issues ad concerns, etc.

Bible Studies

Regular gatherings to explore and discuss the teachings of the Bible.

Prayer Meetings

Opportunities to come together in collective prayer and spiritual reflection.

Retreats

Scheduled retreats and spiritual immersion experiences.

Community Service

Outreach and volunteer initiatives to serve the local community.
